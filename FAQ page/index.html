Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is your return policy?

Return Policy: Items can be returned within 30 days if unused and in original packaging. Contact customer service for details on initiating a return.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting our tracking page and entering your order number and email address. For further assistance, contact our customer service.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

Product Questions

What is the warranty on your products?

Our products come with a one-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. For more details, please refer to our warranty policy or contact customer service.

Can I customize my product?

You can customize our products by visiting our customization page or contacting customer service for help.

What is the return policy for defective products?

Defective products can be returned within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Please contact customer service to initiate the return process.
